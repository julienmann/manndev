What Every Project Includes

Three standards every build ships to,
plus one way to keep it that way.

Inclusivity and Accessibility

A site that only works for some people isn't finished. Every build ships keyboard-navigable, screen-reader compatible, and colour-contrast checked to WCAG 2.2 AA — with reduced motion respected throughout. Accessibility isn't a final pass; it's built in from the first line of code, because the work should reach every visitor, not only the ones on a fast phone in good light.

WCAG 2.2 AA

Ethical Design

No fake urgency, no pre-ticked consent boxes, no surveillance pixels reporting your visitors to networks you never agreed to. Design here prioritises user well-being over conversion tricks — clear choices, honest interfaces, and visitors who feel respected.

No dark patterns

Sustainability and Performance

Lean, hand-coded sites use a fraction of the energy of bloated template builds — multiplied across every visitor, every day. Optimised performance and a lower carbon footprint aren't two different goals. They're the same thing.

Low carbon by default
